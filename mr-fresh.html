The Judging Cat

Mr. Fresh, also known as the Side Eye Cat, refers to an orange cat who makes frequent appearances on the Cat Feeder Camera livestream on the Chinese app Hello Street Cat. The cat is nicknamed Mr. Fresh because he stares at the camera and waits for the viewer to dispense fresh food to eat.
